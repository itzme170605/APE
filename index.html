About


APE - is a free web extension that blocks harmfull or potentially harmfull websites. The number one personal cybersecurity threat is phishing scams designed to steal personal information and accounts. Phishing links are typically delivered via emails, text messages, and pop-ups. Our project, Anti-Phishing Extension (APE), is a browser extension that will reduce the risk posed to users by phishing by alerting the user when they navigate to a known malicious website. The extension should allow users to report a suspicious link, which will be added to a database to help protect the other users of the extension.

what it can do now ?


it ca accept requests to block URL's that our users feel were harmfiul or could be harmful, then undergo a screenoing process from our team and then finally get blocked.

what we plan on adding ?


- a database so outr users ca sign in on other devices and have those webpages blocked - a popup on opening a blocked webpage if they want to continue - connect to a pre-existing database of malicious websiotes to pre- block when extension is added.

Our collective mission is to facilitate safer and more secure web browsing for everyone, and increase awareness of phishing scams.
